## What separates winners from losers

After looking at 50+ DTC consumer brands over the past 5 years, the brands that compound to $10M+ all share:

1. **Founder-led storytelling** (real face, real voice, real reasons)
2. **Single-product clarity** (one hero product, not 20)
3. **Niche obsession** (own a specific use-case, not "skincare" broadly)
4. **Content moat** (50-200+ articles by Year 2 that compound search traffic)
5. **Customer obsession** (founder responds to DMs and emails personally)
6. **Patient brand-building** (no 50% off sales, ever)
7. **Distribution diversification** (DTC + Amazon + retail by Year 3)
8. **Cause / values integration** (1% to EMC fits this exactly)

Becky has 6/8 already in place. The remaining 2 (content moat: in progress; distribution: Amazon already there) build naturally over the next 12 months.

**The brand is well-positioned.** Now it's execution.
