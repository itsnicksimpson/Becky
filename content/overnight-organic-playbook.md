# Overnight Organic — the unfiltered playbook to move 500 units

**Honest framing first.** "Overnight viral" is a survivor-bias myth. What actually happens: brands that "blow up overnight" do 20 specific things across 60-90 days, and ONE of them hits at the right moment. The playbook below is the 20 things. Execute relentlessly and one will hit within 2-3 weeks.

500 units × $20 = $10K. To move all 500, you need ~25,000-50,000 organic visits (at 1-2% conversion). That's hard. Realistic Week 1: 20-80 units. Week 4: 100-200 units. Week 12: rest of inventory + restock plan.

## The 10 highest-leverage organic traffic hacks (ranked by ROI)

### #1 — The Reddit "Drop"
**Estimated traffic: 2,000-50,000 visits in 48h. Sales: 30-300.**

Post a single, well-crafted, fully-disclosed thread in r/SkincareAddiction or r/30PlusSkinCare. NOT a product post — a story/education post that authentically includes Becky.

**The format that hits:**

Title: *"I spent 18 months trying every butt scrub on the market. Here's what I learned. (Founder, will disclose, here to answer questions.)"*

Body: 1,500-2,000 word, deeply useful post that:
1. Discloses founder status in line 1
2. Names competitors honestly (Anese, Buttface, Maelys, Megababe — by name)
3. Includes 3 things you got wrong as a founder
4. Provides a treatment protocol that DOESN'T mention Becky for the first 1,000 words
5. Mentions Becky in a "and yes, here's our product, we built it because of [specific reason]"
6. Ends with: "AMA"

Post Tuesday 9-10am ET. Reply to every comment within 2 hours for the first 6 hours. If thread breaks 500 upvotes, it'll keep climbing for 48 hours.

**Cost:** $0. Risk: medium (post can flop). Win condition: 1 hit = $5K-30K in revenue.

## Site mechanics that convert (the "tomorrow morning" tactical layer)

### Timers and urgency (NOT fake urgency — real)

Add the following to your theme:

**1. Sitewide flash promo banner** — at the top of every page, above the announcement bar:
```
⚡ LAUNCH FLASH: 25% off the first 100 orders. Code FIRST100 expires in [X] hours.
```

This is a REAL countdown — 100 orders only, real expiry. Update copy when 50, 75, 90 orders sold ("Only 10 left at 25% off").

**2. Stock indicator on PDP** — under the price:
```
Only 487 jars left in this batch
```

When you have 500 units total, this is honest. It updates as orders come in (use a Shopify metafield or the inventory level). Honest scarcity converts at 1.4-2x.

**3. Recent purchase popup** (subtle, not spammy):
```
"Amanda from Chicago bought a Booty Scrub 4 minutes ago"
```

Use a free Shopify app like Nudgify or Provely. Show only 2-3 popups per session, only when there are actually recent orders. Lifts conversion 8-12%.

**4. Exit-intent popup** — when someone moves to close the tab:
```
"Wait — get 10% off your first order. Code BUTTNEWBIE."
```

Captures 4-8% of would-be exits. Free Shopify apps: Privy, OptiMonk.

**5. Time-limited cart** — in the slide-in cart:
```
"Your cart is reserved for the next 15 minutes."
```

This is a soft pressure that doesn't piss anyone off but drives 5-8% lift on checkout completion.

### Flash sale mechanics (the actual playbook for 500-unit inventory)

**The "Drop" model — what to do this weekend:**

1. **Day 1 (Saturday):** Announce a "Founder's First Drop" — first 50 jars at $15 (25% off), code FOUNDER15, available 12am Saturday-Sunday only. Push via:
   - Email blast to existing list
   - 1 Reddit comment in r/SkincareAddiction (no spam, just helpful with the code at the end)
   - Instagram stories countdown (8 in a row, every 3 hours)
   - TikTok announcement video

2. **Day 2 (Sunday):** Move 50 units (it WILL sell out — $15 for a $20 best-seller is a real deal). Tweet "SOLD OUT" loud.

3. **Day 3-4 (Mon-Tue):** "Restock!" announcement. Back to $20 base. Push the "missed it? next drop in [X days]" angle to build list.

4. **Week 2:** Run a different test — BUNDLE15 automatic discount (15% off 2+ jars). This pushes AOV from $20 to $36.

5. **Week 3:** A/B test — 100 customers get a free Becky drawstring pouch with $40+ orders, 100 don't. Measure AOV lift.

6. **Week 4:** Mystery flash — random 15-30% off code revealed in newsletter (60% open it = traffic spike).

**Why drops work better than sitewide sales:**
- Sitewide trains customers to wait for the next sale
- Drops train customers to ACT FAST (urgency without devaluation)
- Drops create press hooks ("the brand that sold out in 24 hours")
- Drops build email list (people sign up to be notified of next drop)

### Promo split tests to run (in priority order)

**Test 1 (Week 1):** $20 vs $17.99
- Half of new visitors see $20
- Half see $17.99 (psychological pricing)
- Track conversion rate by group
- Tool: Shopify Plus has this native; or use Convert.com (free trial)
- Hypothesis: $17.99 lifts conversion 8-15%

**Test 2 (Week 2):** Free shipping over $25 vs over $35
- Half see "$25" threshold, half see "$35"
- Measure AOV + conversion
- Hypothesis: $25 threshold = higher conversion, lower AOV. Math the LTV before deciding.

**Test 3 (Week 3):** BOGO 50% vs 25% off site
- Half see "Buy 2, get 50% off the second" (effectively 25% off bundle)
- Half see "25% off entire order"
- BOGO usually wins on AOV (forces 2-jar purchase); flat discount usually wins on conversion. Test which matters more for your inventory math.

**Test 4 (Week 4):** Mystery scratch-card popup vs static 10% off popup
- Mystery scratch = "scratch to reveal 5/10/15/20% off"
- Static = "10% off your first order"
- Mystery typically lifts conversion 30-50%
- Tool: any popup tool with scratch animation (Klaviyo, Privy)

## The "drop pricing" math for 500 units

If your goal is to clear 500 units fast:

| Strategy | Effective $/jar | Total revenue | Sells out in |
|---|---|---|---|
| $20 full price | $20 | $10,000 | 6-12 weeks |
| $20 + 10% off (BUTTNEWBIE) | $18 | $9,000 | 4-8 weeks |
| Drops at $15 (50 units only) + $20 rest | $19.50 avg | $9,750 | 3-6 weeks |
| BOGO 50% off (buy 1 get 1 50% off) | $15/jar | $7,500 | 2-4 weeks |
| $15 sitewide for 7 days | $15 | $7,500 | 2-3 weeks |
| Buy 2 get 1 free (3-pack at $40) | $13.33/jar | $6,666 | 1-2 weeks |

**My recommendation:** Mixed strategy.
- 50 units at $15 in a "drop" (creates buzz)
- 200 units at $20 + bundle discount (drives AOV)
- 250 units at $17 via subscribe-and-save (locks in repeats)
- Total revenue: ~$8,750-9,500
- Sells in 4-7 weeks
- Builds list, repeat customers, social proof for next batch

**Avoid:** Sitewide 50% off. Even though it'd clear inventory in days, you'd have spent your brand capital on what feels like distress sales. Once you've trained customers that 50% off happens, they'll never buy at $20 again.

## What to skip / what's overhyped

- ❌ **Facebook/Instagram ads** at this stage — wait until you have AOV + LTV data from organic (Day 30 minimum)
- ❌ **Paid influencer placements** — $1.5-5K each, low ROI for body care until you've validated organically
- ❌ **Affiliate networks** — set up referrals INTERNAL (Smile.io) instead of through ShareASale
- ❌ **Endless A/B testing the homepage** — pick a layout, ship, learn
- ❌ **Threads / Bluesky / Mastodon** — no real ROI for skincare yet
- ❌ **Building a Shopify mobile app** — not until $1M+ revenue
- ❌ **Hiring a "growth marketer"** — you ARE the growth marketer at this stage; founder-led growth converts best
