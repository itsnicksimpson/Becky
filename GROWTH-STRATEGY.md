## TL;DR

**The thesis:** Becky becomes the encyclopedia of butt skin. Not butt facials. Not booty culture. Not anti-chafing. The single trusted authority on what's happening on the skin between your hip and your knee — what causes it, how to treat it, what to use, when to see a doctor. The Lume playbook ("we own body odor") applied to "we own butt skin texture, appearance, and confidence."

**Why this works:**
1. **Brand permission** — only a butt brand can write this with the right tone.
2. **Topical authority** — Becky can publish 50 posts about butt skin; Bushbalm/Megababe/Lume won't go this narrow.
3. **AEO economics** — LLMs reward narrow taxonomic depth + structured FAQ.
4. **Conversion** — every condition resolves to "exfoliate + the Becky scrub."

The moat compounds. Each post strengthens the "butt skin authority" entity in AI training data. By month 12, "best butt scrub" gets Becky cited because Becky has explained KP-on-butt, folliculitis-on-butt, post-spin-class buttne, postpartum butt skin, anal hyperpigmentation, and 20+ other conditions nobody else owns.

## Phase 1: Research findings (key insights only)

### Brand audit
- **Single SKU** ($20 Booty Scrub, 2 oz) at session start. DTC catalog is anemic.
- **Zero blog content** (the "News" blog had 1 stale URL from 2018).
- **Anonymous founder voice** — biggest missed lever vs Megababe (Katie Sturino) or Anese (founder-led).
- **Amazon best-seller** in butt scrubs — proves product-market fit.
- **Brand voice is sharp:** *"friend who says the quiet part out loud"* — "we eat carbs," "Photoshop is more popular than we thought," "Can we touch your butt?"
- **1% to Every Mother Counts** — built-in halo, especially for postpartum/maternal content.
- **TikTok gap:** at ~5K IG followers, no confirmed TikTok presence with reach. The biggest underpriced channel.

### Competitor whitespace
- **Buttface** (buttface.com) is the most direct threat — has 11 articles including the canonical "butt is biologically different" routine post. They were 12–18 months ahead at session start.
- **Bushbalm** has 177 blog posts — dominates ingrowns/dark spots but isn't butt-specific.
- **Anese** has zero blog content — pure brand+UGC play, ~5+ years of category presence. Massive content moat opportunity vs them.
- **Soft Services' "Mass Index"** (170+ condition pages) is the playbook to copy — applied butt-specifically.
- **Lume** is the AEO benchmark — they're the AI-search default for body odor. Becky should be that for butt skin texture/appearance.
- **Frenshe** has 412 posts but won't write "Buttne vs. Folliculitis." That permission gap is Becky's.

### Search + AI demand
- Becky was **invisible to AI search** at session start. 5 of 5 canonical prompts ("best butt scrub," "how to get rid of butt acne," "why is my butt dark," "how to get smooth butt skin," "best skincare for KP on butt") returned zero Becky mentions.
- "Buttne" is winning on TikTok; "butt acne" is winning on Google. Becky needs both.
- The under-served lifecycle is **pre-vacation / pre-event** — seasonally massive, low difficulty, recurring.
- The under-served cohort is **dark-skin / POC** butt-care content.
- Brand-comparison content ("Anese vs Becky," "Becky vs Buttface") is open territory.

## Phase 2: The strategy framework

### Information Architecture


**Navigation (5-item main menu):** Shop · Routine · The Atlas · Reviews · About

**Footer (8 items):** The Booty Atlas · The Routine · FAQ · Reviews · About · Search · Refund Policy · Terms of Service

### SEO + AEO + GEO playbook

**SEO (Google):**
- Pillar-cluster architecture: 1 pillar (Routine) + 19 supporting articles, all interlinked
- Every article: SEO title (~55 chars), meta description (~155 chars), TL;DR (multi-line summary), reading time
- Internal links: every article cross-references 2-3 others + the PDP
- Junip review schema on PDP (waiting on real data)
- Sitemap auto-generated by Shopify

**AEO (Answer Engines: ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Google AI Overviews):**
- **FAQPage schema** sitewide via `snippets/faq-schema-json-ld.liquid` — auto-emits when any page has `becky.faq_entries` metafield set
- **Article schema** with author, datePublished, dateModified, keywords, wordCount via `snippets/schema-json-ld.liquid`
- **Product schema** with offers, aggregateRating (from metafield), brand
- **Organization + WebSite + BreadcrumbList** on every page
- Comparison content ("Becky vs X") — LLMs love side-by-side tables
- Differential diagnosis content ("Buttne vs. Folliculitis vs. KP") — LLMs love disambiguation

**GEO (Generative Engine Optimization — getting cited by LLMs):**
- E-E-A-T signals: author bylines, dermatologist quotes (TODO partnership), references to clinical literature, founder transparency
- Third-party citations needed: PR placements in NBC Select, Yahoo Shopping, Marie Claire, Refinery29, Glamour (LLMs cite editorial commerce heavily)
- Reddit founder presence (non-spammy AMAs in r/SkincareAddiction, r/30PlusSkinCare, r/keratosis_pilaris)
- Schema-marked everything (already done)

### Conversion layer

**In every blog article:**
- TL;DR at top (matches AI engines' summary preference)
- 1+ internal link to PDP within first 500 words
- 2+ internal links to other Atlas articles
- Strong end-CTA with link to `/products/booty-scrub`
- "Read next" cross-links

**On the PDP:**
- 9 structured metafields driving rich UI: FAQ (10 entries → FAQPage schema), key ingredients (5 with benefits), best-for (7 conditions), use-steps (5 steps), results timeline (5 milestones), brand proof (6 trust signals)
- ⚠️ Placeholder `review_count: 1247` / `rating_value: 4.7` — REPLACE with real Junip data
- Strong product description with ingredient story, results timeline, brand truth, internal link to Routine

**On the new home:**
- Hero: "Hi, we're Becky. The skin no one talks about deserves a routine."
- Featured product anchor
- 3 benefit blocks (Walnut+Rosehip, Vegan+Cruelty-free, 1% to EMC)
- 3 testimonial blocks
- Story section ("Let's talk about your booty" classic copy)
- Featured Atlas posts (3 + view-all)
- Newsletter CTA: "Can we touch your butt?"

### Programmatic SEO loops (the moat that compounds)

Four templated patterns that can spawn 30-100+ articles:

1. **`[Condition] on the butt`** — Buttne, KP, ingrowns, stretch marks, strawberry skin, cellulite, anal hyperpigmentation, razor bumps, dryness, eczema, psoriasis, folliculitis variants
2. **`Butt acne after [trigger]`** — Spin class (✅ done), Peloton, hot yoga, Pilates, running, swimming, sitting all day, tight jeans, polyester underwear, fabric softener, sleeping naked
3. **`Becky vs [competitor]`** — Anese (✅), Buttface (✅), Maelys B-Tight, Megababe Le Tush, Truly Berry Cheeky, Sol de Janeiro Bum Bum, generic CeraVe SA
4. **`[Skin type] butt routine`** — Sensitive (✅), Acne-prone, Dry, Oily, KP-prone, Postpartum (✅), Aging/perimenopause, Dark skin tones
5. **`How to prep your butt for [event]`** — Swimsuit (✅), Wedding, Honeymoon, Photoshoot, Lingerie, First date, Naked retreat

Each template can spawn 8-12 articles. Programmatic = low marginal cost once a pattern is proven.

## Schema templates reference

### Article schema (auto-emitted on every article page)
```json
{
  "@context": "https://schema.org",
  "@type": "Article",
  "headline": "[title]",
  "description": "[summary]",
  "image": {"@type":"ImageObject", "url":"[hero image]"},
  "author": {"@type":"Person", "name":"[author or The Becky Team]"},
  "publisher": {"@id":"hibecky.com/#organization"},
  "datePublished": "[ISO date]",
  "dateModified": "[ISO date]",
  "articleSection": "The Booty Atlas",
  "keywords": "[comma-separated tags]",
  "wordCount": "[word count]"
}
```

### FAQPage schema (auto-emitted when becky.faq_entries metafield set)
```json
{
  "@context": "https://schema.org",
  "@type": "FAQPage",
  "mainEntity": [
    {
      "@type": "Question",
      "name": "[question]",
      "acceptedAnswer": {"@type":"Answer","text":"[answer]"}
    }
  ]
}
```

### Product schema (auto-emitted on PDPs)
```json
{
  "@context": "https://schema.org",
  "@type": "Product",
  "name": "Booty Scrub",
  "brand": {"@type":"Brand","name":"Becky"},
  "image": ["[image urls]"],
  "aggregateRating": {
    "@type": "AggregateRating",
    "ratingValue": "[from metafield]",
    "reviewCount": "[from metafield]"
  },
  "offers": [
    {"@type":"Offer", "price":"20.00", "priceCurrency":"USD", "availability":"InStock"}
  ]
}
```
